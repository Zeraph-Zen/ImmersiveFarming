Horse Carts
Horse power!

Instead of doing all the work by yourself, you can use a horse to help you till dirt, plant and fertilize seeds. The first step is crafting wheels.
<&wheel>
After you can make a plow, which you can attach to a horse, load with some hoes and use to till dirt.
<&plow>
Finally, you can crafting a sowing machine, which will help you sow and fertilize seeds. The upper compartment is for seeds while the bottom is for fertilizer.
<&sower>
